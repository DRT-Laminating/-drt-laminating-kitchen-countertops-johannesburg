### 💡 Why Granite Is No Longer the Default

Granite used to be the top choice for **kitchen tops**. But real-life use has revealed some drawbacks.

❌ Expensive material and installation  
❌ Requires sealing and ongoing maintenance  
❌ Can crack or stain over time  
❌ Limited flexibility once installed  

Today, more people are asking a smarter question:  
**What actually works long-term?**

---

### 🚀 What’s Replacing Granite in 2026?

Modern kitchens are shifting toward:

✅ **Postform countertops**  
✅ **Formica countertops**  
✅ **Flat-pressed countertops**  

These options offer the same visual appeal — without the high cost and maintenance.
